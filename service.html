Frequently Asked Questions

Your level of involvement is entirely up to you. Some clients prefer to be involved in every decision, while others trust us to manage the details.

How do I start an interior design project with you?

Reach out via our contact page to schedule a consultation. We’ll guide you through design goals, timelines, and budget planning.

Can you redesign a space using my existing furniture?

Absolutely! We specialize in blending existing pieces with new design elements to elevate your space while preserving what you love.

Do you offer full-house interior design services?

Yes, we manage full-home transformations from layout planning to final styling, working closely with contractors and artisans.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope, but most single-room projects take 4–6 weeks. Full-house designs may range from 8 to 16 weeks.

What design styles do you specialize in?

We tailor each space to your preferences—modern, minimalist, bohemian, luxurious, or eclectic. Your vision guides our design.
