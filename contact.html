FAQ:

What interior design services do you offer?
We provide a comprehensive range of services including residential and commercial space planning, bespoke furniture design, 3D architectural visualization, material procurement, and end-to-end project management.
How does the design process work from start to finish?
Our journey begins with a discovery consultation to understand your vision. We move into conceptual design with mood boards, then technical drafting and 3D rendering. Once approved, we handle the execution, installation, and final styling.
How long does an interior design project typically take?
Timelines vary based on complexity. A single-room styling project may take 4 to 6 weeks, while a full-scale renovation for a luxury home or office can span anywhere from 3 to 6 months.
Do you work with existing furniture and decor?
Absolutely. We can integrate your cherished pieces into the new design. Our goal is to create a cohesive space that reflects your personality, often by repurposing or reupholstering your current items to fit the updated aesthetic.
How do you handle project budgets and cost estimates?
Transparency is key to our process. We establish a clear budget during the initial phase and provide detailed itemized estimates. We use our industry connections to get the best value without compromising on the quality of materials or craftsmanship.
Can I see the design in 3D before the work begins?
Yes, we provide high-fidelity 3D renderings as a standard part of our design package. This allows you to "walk through" your space and visualize every texture, color, and lighting detail before any physical work starts on-site.
